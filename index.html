Understanding the Columns
  • ShopID: A unique identifier from the company’s internal system. Use this ID to cross‑reference the shop in other databases.
  • AccountName: The full name of the pizzeria as it appears in your records. This is the store name you’ll mention in outreach.
  • BillingCity & BillingZip: The city and ZIP code where the shop operates. These help you confirm you’re targeting the right location.
  • Website: The URL of the shop’s own website, if one exists. Blank values indicate the business does not have a site listed.
  • HasWebsite: True if Google Places returned a website for the shop; otherwise False. Shops without a site are prime candidates for Slice’s website package.
  • DirectOrdering: True when the shop’s website offers its own ordering (not just links to DoorDash/GrubHub). If False, the business relies solely on third‑party apps and may benefit from Slice’s integrated ordering and POS.
  • Note: Additional context explaining why a shop is flagged—e.g., “No website” or “Third‑party only.” Use this field to tailor your outreach.
